A Letter to You

My darling, on this beautiful day, I want you to know just how deeply you are loved. Every single day you bring light into my life in ways I never knew were possible. You are my peace, my joy, and my greatest blessing. I am so grateful God placed you in my world. May this new year of your life overflow with everything your heart desires — good health, infinite happiness, and all the love you so freely give to others. You deserve every beautiful thing life has to offer, and I pray this year exceeds your every dream.

Today belongs to you. Celebrate yourself — every single piece of who you are.

— With all my love, always ♡
